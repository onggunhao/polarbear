ABOUT

This network represents a random 0.05% sample of tweets collected during the 2010 Congressional midterm elections.


Nodes represent twitter users
(red = republican, blue = democrat).

Edges represent some form of interaction
(RT and @mention)

This visualization was presented by Josh Valdez and Daniel Ong, as part of the data visualization course taught by Jeffrey Heer at Stanford
The dataset used for this project is from the ICWSM 2011 dataset, from the CNETS group at Indiana University.
